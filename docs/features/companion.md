# Virtual Companion

A fully animated character appears on your screen, providing visual feedback during AI interactions. The companion responds with expressions and gestures, making your browsing experience more interactive and engaging.

## What is the Virtual Companion?

The companion is an animated character model that:

- Shows different states (idle, thinking, speaking)
- Provides lip-synced speech during Text-to-Speech playback
- Can be positioned anywhere on screen via dragging

## Display Modes

### Full Body Mode

Shows the entire character model:

- Complete animations and gestures
- More immersive visual presence
- Best for larger screens
- Default mode

### Portrait Mode

Shows upper body only:

- Focuses on face and expressions
- Better for laptops or smaller displays
- Enable in [UI Settings](../configuration/ui-settings.md)

## Positioning

### Dragging

Click and hold the companion to drag it anywhere on screen:

- Works in both full body and portrait modes
- Position is saved automatically when using "Last Location" preset

### Position Presets

Choose from predefined positions in [UI Settings](../configuration/ui-settings.md):

- **Bottom Right** - Default chatbot position
- **Bottom Left** - Mirror of bottom right
- **Bottom Center** - Centered at bottom
- **Top Right** - Upper right corner
- **Top Left** - Upper left corner
- **Top Center** - Centered at top
- **Center** - Large centered view for development
- **Last Location** - Remembers your custom drag position

Each preset automatically adjusts for full body and portrait modes.

## Interaction

### Drag Content to Companion

Drag text or images from webpages directly onto the companion to start a chat with that content as context.

## Customization

All customization options are in [UI Settings](../configuration/ui-settings.md):

- **Enable/Disable** - Toggle companion on/off
- **Display Mode** - Full body or portrait
- **Position** - Choose preset or drag to custom location
- **FPS Limit** - Control animation performance

## Performance

The companion is optimized for performance:

- Adjustable FPS limits (30/60/90/native)
- Lower FPS settings save battery on laptops
- Only active in current tab
